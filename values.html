Our Core Values

Our ministry is founded upon principles that guide our worship, service, and outreach. These core values define who we are as a church family:

PRAYER

We are a house of prayer, believing in the power of direct and consistent communication with God to guide every aspect of our lives and ministry.

APOSTOLIC FAITH

We uphold the foundational teachings and practices of the early apostles, emphasizing divine healing, the operation of spiritual gifts, and uncompromising truth.

UNITY & LOVE

We commit to fostering an environment of genuine love, acceptance, and unity among all members, reflecting Christ's command to love one another.

EVANGELISM

We are dedicated to actively spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ to our local community and the world, fulfilling the Great Commission.

EXCELLENCE

We strive for excellence in all our services, ministries, and administration, honoring God with the highest standards of quality and dedication.

SERVICE & OUTREACH

We believe in practical ministry, serving the needs of the broken, marginalized, and vulnerable, demonstrating God's compassion through tangible acts of service.
