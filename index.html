Welcome to International Saint John's Apostolic Faith Mission Church!

International Saint John's Apostolic Faith Mission Church is a non-profit apostolic church dedicated to spiritual growth and community support. We are more than just a place of worship. We are a home for all who seek God's presence.

We offer spiritual consultations every Saturday, where you can receive guidance, prayer, and personal ministry. On Sundays, we gather for a powerful, spirit-filled worship service filled with love, faith, and unity.

We would be honored to welcome you into our church family. When you arrive, feel free to ask for Bishop M. Gcebe or LadyBishop (Dr) N. Gcebe our dedicated church leaders who are always ready to greet and guide you. All are welcome. We can't wait to meet you!
