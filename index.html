Welcome to International Saint John's Apostolic Faith Mission Church!

International Saint John's Apostolic Faith Mission Church is a non-profit apostolic church dedicated to spiritual growth and community support. We are more than just a place of worship. We strive to be a welcoming family where all are accepted and encouraged to grow in their faith.

We offer spiritual consultations every **Saturday** at 10 AM, where you can receive guidance, prayer, and personal ministry. On **Sundays**, we gather for a powerful, spirit-filled worship service filled with praise, preaching, and communion.

We would be honored to welcome you into our church family. When you arrive, feel free to ask for Bishop M. Gcebe or LadyBishop (Dr) N. Gcebe, our dedicated church leaders who are always ready to meet and greet new members.
