Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

The mission of International St John's Apostolic Faith Mission Church is to **spread the Apostolic Gospel of Jesus Christ** globally, emphasizing the power of the Holy Spirit, divine healing, and personal holiness. We are committed to nurturing spiritual growth, fostering genuine community, and serving humanity with compassion and integrity.

**We strive to:**

  • Disciple believers for effective ministry and leadership.
  • Provide holistic spiritual and emotional care for all who seek guidance.
  • Engage in active community outreach and social upliftment programs.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a **powerful, unified, and influential Apostolic movement** recognized for its unwavering commitment to biblical truth and impactful community service. We see a church body that is vibrant, growing, and instrumental in transforming lives and communities throughout Southern Africa and beyond.

**Our goal is to:**

  • Establish a network of faith-based institutions, including Chaplaincy and Bible Academy training centers.
  • Empower the next generation of spiritual leaders through education and mentorship.
  • Be a beacon of hope and a sanctuary for spiritual restoration and healing.
