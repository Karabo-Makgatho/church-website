The International Saint John Church BDC assists the Archbishops, bishops in areas of faith and morals. It collaborates with the our church Bible Academy of the International Saint John Church through consultation and evaluation, providing expertise and guidance concerning the theological matters, doctrinal matters, moral and bible training issues that confront our Church.
